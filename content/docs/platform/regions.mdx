# Regions

Each workspace is tied to a specific region, and is deployed in that region's [ZeroKMS instance](/docs/kms).

## Supported ZeroKMS regions

- Asia Pacific (Sydney)
- Europe (Frankfurt)
- Europe (Ireland)
- US East (N. Virginia)
- US East (Ohio)
- US West (N. California)
- US West (Oregon)

## Requesting a new region

If you need a region that is not listed above, please contact us at [support@cipherstash.com](mailto:support@cipherstash.com).
