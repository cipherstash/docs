Each workspace is tied to a specific region and is deployed in that region's [ZeroKMS instance](/stack/cipherstash/kms).

## Supported ZeroKMS regions

ZeroKMS is deployed on AWS only. A region identifier forms part of your workspace CRN, for example `crn:ap-southeast-2.aws:your-workspace-id`.
